Spot The Court

About Our Final Project in Deep Learning Internship

Spot-The-Court is our final project for Computer Science B.Sc. degree
The system we made is a basketball games video analysis
The platform's end-users is all of the basketball community – coaches, players, commentators, analysts and just ordinary basketball fans

There are 4 main features presented as part of the system:

  • Player recognition and team-classification
  • Ball tracker
  • Classification of players actions
  • Post-game statistics report
We hope you enjoy using the system and wish you all the best!
